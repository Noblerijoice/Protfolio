About

Hi, I'm Noblerijoice T, a Game Developer and Software Engineer from Thevekkal, Ernakulam. I completed my schooling at St. Joseph HSS Kizhakkambalam and earned a diploma in Automobile Engineering. While engineering was my path, programming has always been my passion.

Since high school, I taught myself Python, building mini platformer games and coding famous algorithms for fun. When I realized Python wasn’t ideal for large-scale 3D games, I learned C# and started creating games in Unity, overcoming challenges with YouTube tutorials and documentation.

My passion led me to the industry, where I joined ARCHFIEND STUDIO PVT LTD and contributed to *Pixel X Racer: Retro Racer*. I also assisted on several smaller game projects. Currently, I’m working at Metashot, helping develop an exciting cricket game, *Metashot*.

I’m driven by curiosity and a love for creating immersive experiences through code. Let’s connect to build something extraordinary!
